Introduction¶

This dataset comes from IMDB and contains information around 10,800 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings, revenue, runtime and budget. In this project, we'll be answering the following questions: Which genres are most popular from year to year? What kinds of properties are associated with movies that are succesful(revenue>budget)?
